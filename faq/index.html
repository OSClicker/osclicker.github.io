What is OSClicker?

OSClicker is an advanced macro tool for Windows, Mac, and GNU/Linux. It is designed to make the clicks seem as realistic as possible by using click patterns and making slight mouse movements with every click.

Does it support hotkeys?

Yes! You can set your own custom hotkeys to start/stop clicking.
